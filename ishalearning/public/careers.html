Careers

Subject Matter Experts
If you have experience of working in the academia or in the apparel or related sectors, we have full time, part time, freelance opportunities as a Subject Matter Expert in Apparel Merchandising, Apparel Product Development, Apparel Production etc. You will collaborate with Instructional Designers, Multi Media Professionals, Story Board Creators to create compelling training and learning material in print and video formats. If this interests you, please write to us with your detailed CV at info@ishalearning.com

Online Trainers
ISHA Learning offers Online Training that leverages Digital Content + Experienced Trainers + Career Coaches to plug the skill gaps in working professionals as well as students. If you enjoy training and have experience working in the academia or in the apparel and related sectors, you can work with us an Online Trainer enabling young professionals and students realize their potential and contribute to their work places more effectively. We have full time,part time and freelance opportunities. If this interests you, please write to us with your detailed CV at info@ishalearning.com

Industry Mentors
If you have many years of working in the apparel,retail or related sectors and would like to mentor young professionals in your free time,here is an opportunity that will enable you to impact many, many young professionals as they find their feet in the industry. You can specify the times when you are available during a week or month and we will connect you with young professionals who have signed up for mentoring through our online platform. You can interacting with them in audio, video, chat modes. Apart from the satisfaction of providing guidance to someone, you also get paid handsomely for it. You can spend as little as one hour a month or you could be available full time. If this interests you, please write to us with your detailed CV at info@ishalearning.com

Content Writers
If you have been teaching subjects related to apparel, fashion, retail or other related sectors or have working in the industry, and find writing fulfilling, you will find our content writing positions interesting. Content writing positions involve creating learning and training content for various processes in the apparel value chain including basic content, session plans, assignments,assessment plans, quiz, case studies etc. We have full time,part time and freelance opportunities. If this interests you, please write to us with your detailed CV at info@ishalearning.com
